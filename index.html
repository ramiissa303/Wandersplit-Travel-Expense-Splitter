What it solves

Every pain point, eliminated.

From a weekend hike to a month-long expedition, Wandersplit handles every money moment with grace.

No More Spreadsheets

Ditch the cobbled-together Excel file. Wandersplit's live dashboard replaces every manual ledger your group ever suffered through.

Automatic Debt Minimization

Our settlement engine reduces the number of transactions to the mathematical minimum, so fewer people have to pay each other back.

Multi-Currency Support

USD, EUR, GBP, JOD, JPY and more. Log expenses in whichever currency you paid in, and Wandersplit keeps it clean and separate.

Instant Settlements

Tap recalculate and see exactly who pays whom in seconds. Clear, direct, and indisputable — the trip ends with everyone at peace.

Custom Split Rules

Not everyone shares every expense. Choose exactly who splits each cost — split the hotel with five but the romantic dinner with two.

Persistent Memory

Your trip data lives in your browser. Refresh, close the tab, come back three days later — everything is exactly where you left it.

How it works

Three steps to total clarity.

01

Add your travel crew

Type in the names of everyone on the trip. Takes ten seconds. No accounts, no sign-ups, no email confirmation.

02

Record expenses as you go

Who paid for the riad? Who covered the taxi? Log each expense live, choose the currency, and decide who it should be split between.

03

Wandersplit settles the debts

Hit recalculate. Our engine computes the minimum number of transfers needed to make everyone perfectly even. Clean, final, done.
